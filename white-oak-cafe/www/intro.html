Register or Continue As Guest

The first interactive page you encounter will require you to "Login" (discussed in an upcoming slide), "Register" or "Continue as Guest". The preferred option is to register since there is added benefits. Plus, by registering, your login information can be used by all the mobile applications created by J&K Media Solutions.

To "Register" or "Continue As Guest", look for the green buttons below on the Login page.

Registration

To register, fill out the required information and click the green "Register" button. You will get prompted if a field isn't filled out correctly.

Login

After you have registered, you will be prompted to "Login".

To login, fill out the email address and password you used to register, then click the blue "Login" button.

If you check the "Remember Me" checkbox you will not be prompted to login when open the app, unless you either logout or reinstall the app.


Reset Password

If you forgot your password, don't worry, just look for the blue "Reset Password" button on the Login page.

Once clicked, you will be prompted to enter your registered email address. An email will get sent with a new password. We can't retreive and send you the lost password since all stored passwords are encrypted.

Navigation

To navigate to differt parts of the app, use the icon in the upper right corner of the app. Once clicked, a menu will drop down with a list of pages to visit.


User Settings

To view your user settings, click the navigation icon in the upper right corner of the app, then choose "Settings". From this page you can either change your password or personal information. Fill out the text and hit the update button.

Home

The "Home" screen is where you will find out the information about your restaurant hours and location. You will be able to start shopping by clicking the "Order Online" button. You can even call the restaurant by clicking the green button with their phone number on it. You will be able to access your "Previous Orders" so you can quickly order your food.


Ordering - Categories

You can view the menu by touching the "Order Online" button from the "Home" screen, or you can use the navigation icon in the upper right corner and selecting "Menu". Once on the menu, you can choose the category of food you would like, by clicking it. Once clicked, the category will expand to list all the available items for that category.


Ordering - Items

Once you have found the item you want, click the heading to expand all the available condiments. If this item is a "combo" meal, you will be prompted to select the appropriate meal options.


Ordering - Add To Cart

Once you have added all the condiments or meal options for your item, you can adjust the quantity that you want by clicking the "+" (blue) and "-" (red) buttons. When you have the desired quantity, click the green "Add To Cart" button. Once clicked your item will be placed in the cart and await checkout.


Ordering - View Cart

You can view the cart at anytime during your order by clicking on the cart idon in the upper left of the application. The cart icon in the upper left, displays the price and number of items you have in your cart. When clicking the icon, the cart detail page will be displayed. This page will show your your order total, allow you to remove items, and allow you to update quantities of items. You can also add special notes for your order.


Ordering - Checkout

After you have added all your items, from the "View Cart" screen, choose the green "Checkout" button. You will then be prompted to choose if you want to "Dine In", "Pick Up", or "Delivery" (subject to restaurant availability). Next, review your order to make sure it is correct. Then choose your payment method of at the store or through the app with your credit/debit card.


Ordering - Checkout cont'd

Check your contact information to make sure it is correct. If you will be using this order often, make sure to give it a name so you can re-order these items with the click of a button. Once satisfied that your information is correct and you have entered an order name, click the blue "Place Secure Order" button to submit your order to the restaurant and await for the confirmation page.


Previous Orders

If you gave your orders a name at checkout, you can view them pretty easily. From the "Home" screen, click the blue "Previous Orders" button, or from the navigation icon in the upper right corner, choose "Previous Orders". If you have orders saved, you can easily click the green "Checkout" button to take you to the check out screen. Review your order details and contact information, and you are ready to check out.


Complete
